Wondering in the woods

Best hikes for the Fall

Top 3 places to hike

Starved Rock State Park
Giant City State Park
Pere Marquette State Park

There is a term to describe the color changing of the leaves during the Fall: Foliage, an english term that comes from the french “feuillage”.
So during the fall you can fully appreciate long hikes while admiring the foliage. The woods put their best outfits on with red, orange, yellow leaves to charm everyone.
There are a lot of parks in the United States when you can also challenge yourself with demanding hikes if you are down for it.


In Starved Rock State Park (links for parks' information on the right side) there are 13 miles of hiking trails to discover, spacing from amateur hiking to expert routes.
Giant City State Park offers various actvities and provides a designed trail for disabled visitors so everyone can enjoy the Fall season and its colors.
Instead for bikers, Pere Marquette Park offers a wonderful 20 miles bike trail to appreciate amazing views.

Silvia Vorano, September 2022
